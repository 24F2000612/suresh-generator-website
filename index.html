Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about generators and our services

What capacity generator do I need for my home/business?
The capacity depends on your total power requirement. For homes, typically 7.5-25 kVA is sufficient. For factories and commercial establishments, we recommend 50 kVA and above. Contact us for a free site survey and expert consultation.
Do you provide installation services?
Yes! We provide complete installation services including site preparation, generator setup, electrical connections, and commissioning. Our expert technicians ensure proper installation following all safety standards.
What is the warranty period on generators?
All our generators come with manufacturer's warranty ranging from 2-3 years depending on the model. We also offer extended warranty options and comprehensive Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC).
Do you offer 24/7 emergency service?
Absolutely! We provide round-the-clock emergency service 365 days a year. Just call +91 9829325775 anytime and our technicians will reach your location promptly to resolve any generator issues.
Are generators available on rent?
Yes! We offer both short-term and long-term generator rental services. All capacities are available with delivery, installation, fuel management, and maintenance included in the rental package.
What areas do you serve?
We are based in Neemrana and serve entire Rajasthan including Alwar, Jaipur, Bhiwadi, Dharuhera, and surrounding areas. Contact us for service availability in your area.
