Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What capacity generator do I need?
For homes: 7.5-25 kVA is typically sufficient. For businesses and factories: 50 kVA and above depending on load. We provide FREE site surveys to assess your exact power requirements and recommend the most suitable generator capacity.
Do you provide installation services?
Yes! We provide complete installation service including electrical connections, ATS panel setup, commissioning, operator training, and all safety compliance documentation. Our expert technicians ensure smooth and professional installation.
What warranty is provided?
All generators come with 2-3 years comprehensive manufacturer warranty covering parts and service. Extended warranty options and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) are available for long-term peace of mind and coverage.
Is 24/7 emergency service available?
Absolutely! Call +91 9829325775 anytime. Our experienced technicians respond promptly 365 days a year for any emergency situations. We understand power is critical for your operations and provide rapid response support.
Are generators available on rent?
Yes! Short-term and long-term rental options available for all capacity ranges. Rental includes delivery, installation, fuel supply, maintenance, and 24/7 support. Flexible payment terms available to suit your business needs.
