Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What capacity generator do I need for my requirement?
For homes: 7.5-25 kVA depending on load. For businesses: 50 kVA and above. We provide FREE site surveys to determine exact requirements based on your electrical load, future expansion plans, and backup duration needs.
Do you provide installation and commissioning services?
Yes! We provide complete turnkey solutions including generator installation, electrical connections, ATS panel setup, fuel system, commissioning, load testing, operator training, and documentation - all done by certified technicians.
What warranty is provided with the generators?
We offer 2-3 years manufacturer warranty depending on the brand and model. Extended warranty options are available. All warranties are comprehensive covering parts, labor, and service. AMC contracts available post-warranty period.
Is 24/7 emergency service really available?
Absolutely! Call +91 9829325775 anytime day or night. We maintain dedicated emergency response teams available 365 days/year nationwide. Average response time: Within 4 hours for emergency breakdowns.
Do you serve locations outside Neemrana?
Yes! We serve all major cities across North, South, East & West India. Our network includes Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, Ahmedabad, Kolkata, and 50+ other cities with complete sales and service support.
What payment options are available?
We offer flexible payment options including: Direct bank transfer, cheque payments, installment plans (with approved credit), financing through partner banks, and trade-in options for old generators. GST invoices provided for all transactions.
Are spare parts readily available?
Yes! We maintain comprehensive inventory of genuine spare parts for all major brands. Parts availability within 24-48 hours for common items. We use only OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts to ensure optimal performance and warranty validity.
What is included in AMC contracts?
AMC includes: Scheduled preventive maintenance (monthly/quarterly), free oil changes, filter replacements, coolant top-ups, battery checks, load testing, emergency breakdown support, priority service, detailed maintenance reports, and genuine spare parts at discounted rates.
