Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our products and services

What capacity generator do I need for my requirements?
For residential needs: 7.5-25 kVA is typically sufficient for homes and small offices. For businesses and factories: 50 kVA and above depending on your load requirements. We provide FREE comprehensive site surveys to assess your exact power requirements and recommend the most suitable generator capacity for optimal performance and cost-efficiency.
Do you provide complete installation services?
Yes! We provide comprehensive installation services including all electrical connections, ATS (Automatic Transfer Switch) panel setup, load testing, commissioning, operator training, and complete safety compliance documentation. Our expert technicians ensure smooth, professional installation with minimal disruption to your operations.
What warranty coverage is provided with generators?
All our generators come with 2-3 years comprehensive manufacturer warranty covering parts, service, and labor. Extended warranty options and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) are available for long-term peace of mind and coverage. We also provide genuine spare parts with warranty for all repairs and maintenance work.
Is 24/7 emergency service really available?
Absolutely! Call +91 9829325775 anytime, day or night. Our experienced technicians are on standby and respond promptly 365 days a year for any emergency situations. We understand that power is critical for your operations, and we provide rapid response support with well-stocked service vehicles ready to reach you quickly.
Are generators available for rent on short notice?
Yes! Short-term and long-term rental options are available for all capacity ranges with quick mobilization. Rental services include delivery, professional installation, fuel supply coordination, regular maintenance, and 24/7 technical support. Flexible payment terms are available to suit your business needs and budget constraints.
What payment and financing options are available?
We offer flexible payment options including cash, bank transfers, cheques, and easy financing plans. For corporate clients, we provide credit facilities and customized payment schedules. Special financing arrangements can be made for large projects and bulk orders. Contact us to discuss the best payment plan for your requirements.
